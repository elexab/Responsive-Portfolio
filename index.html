About Me

Hello, my name is Elexa Evans and I am currently enrolled in the SMU Boot Camp to become a Full Stack Developer. Every since I was younger, I always enjoyed working with computers. As I got older, that passion drew me to want to become a Developer and build websites and apps. My original major in college was Computer Science at The University of North Texas. Two years into my degree plan, I soon realized that I was not grasping the programming concepts needed for me to successfully become a Developer. This led me to decided to change my major.

I decided to change my major to Information Science and Applied Technolody with a concentration in Project and Knowledge Management in hopes to become an IT Project Manager. After graduating in December 2018, I found myself still searching for employment in the Project Management field six months later. I was then presented with this opportunity to attend this Boot Camp and have the potential to get employed upon successfully completed it. I am extremely grateful that I was provided this opportunity and hope that I get hired by Tek Systems.
